Pre-Launch & Launch Marketing Plan

Four-Week Launch Strategy

Each phase builds momentum toward a successful launch

⚡
Week 01

Foundation & Funnel Setup

Establish the technical infrastructure and begin building awareness with countdown timers, email capture, and initial social content.

📸
Week 02

Content & Product Focus

Showcase the hero product through professional content creation, product photography, and strategic social media campaigns.

📧
Week 03

Campaign Build-Out

Develop nurture sequences, expand product offerings, and create exclusivity through subscriber-only access messaging.

🚀
Week 04

Launch Execution

Execute the full launch sequence with final testing, go-live coordination, and strategic email marketing pushes.

Days 1-7

Foundation & Funnel Setup

Building the technical foundation and starting to generate buzz

  • Update countdown clock on website to create urgency
  • Add and connect signup form to email marketing platform
  • Write confirmation email and QA test complete signup flow
  • Draft 7–10 social posts featuring teasers and brand vision
  • Design 5–6 Instagram and TikTok story templates
  • Begin consistent daily posting with clear signup CTAs

Setting up the foundation with professional brand assets and strategic planning

Professional product photography and content creation drive engagement

Days 8-14

Content & Product Focus

Showcasing the hero product through compelling visuals and content

  • Finalize hero product PDP with professional photos, copy, and SKU setup
  • Conduct product photoshoot and collect supporting content assets
  • Design graphics and videos highlighting countdown and signup CTA
  • Schedule one full week of content in advance for consistency
  • Post engaging teaser reel/TikTok with strong signup call-to-action
Days 15-21

Campaign Build-Out

Expanding the product line and creating subscriber exclusivity

  • Write and design 2 pre-launch nurture emails for subscriber engagement
  • Build 1–2 additional product detail pages to offer variety
  • Push exclusivity messaging: 'Only subscribers get first access'
  • Test small paid advertising campaign ($20–50) to boost signups

Building anticipation through exclusive access and strategic campaign messaging

Final preparations and launch execution for maximum impact

Days 22-30

Launch Execution

Go-live coordination and strategic email marketing sequence

  • Write and schedule launch announcement and last chance emails
  • Conduct final QA testing: checkout flow, PDPs, mobile performance
  • Execute go-live sequence for website and all products
  • Maintain daily posting schedule throughout launch window
  • Deploy email sequence: launch → reminder → last chance

Expected Outcomes

Audience Growth
Consistent anticipation and engagement throughout the entire pre-launch period
Quality Subscribers
Strong, engaged subscriber list primed and ready for launch conversion
Technical Excellence
Clean, tested launch experience with optimized user journey
Sales Performance
Maximum conversion rates during the critical launch window

This strategic approach ensures Money Making Mindset makes a powerful, memorable market entry that converts anticipation into sales.
